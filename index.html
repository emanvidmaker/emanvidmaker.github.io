Emanvidmaker


Emanvidmaker is a Indie Game Developer. Created by Emanuel Acosta and based in Ponce, Puerto Rico.

Mission

Our vision is to create memorable and novel impactful experiences for our players.

Vision

A world where Gamers have good games to talk about. In a created in sustainable way.

Emanuel Acosta Gonzalez

He been making games since he was 12 years old. As he grew older, he noticed a lack of game development studios in Puerto Rico.

Struggling to seek a job in the industry, and sadden by the low quality games published by AAA studios, he founded Emanvidmaker (DBA) in 2024. Where He took to his hands to develop and to publish his own games.

Description

Jump down the Bottomless Pit, and find out if there's an end to it. In this fall, you got to avoid the pillars heading your way. How long will you survive?

History

This game was made for Ludum Dare 48 back in 2021. I decided to make a more complete, version and publish it to different app stores. So I Kept working on it while i studied programming, and showed it off at different cons. And after several code rewritings and different engine ports i have finally made something im proud of.

About Emanvidmaker

Emanvidmaker is a Indie developer based in Ponce, Puerto Rico. Our vision is to give a sense of community and unity for our players. Our mission is creating memorable experiences for our players, while bringing sustainable game development jobs and opportunities to the island. Created by Emanuel Acosta Gonzalez. Hes been making games since he was 12 and needed a platform to publish his game, so he founded Emanvidmaker (DBA) in 2024.

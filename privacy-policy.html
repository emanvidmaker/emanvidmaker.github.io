Privacy Policy for Emanvidmaker

Effective Date: 23 November 2024

Emanvidmaker ("we,” “our,” or “us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information across all our mobile apps and games ("Services"). By using our Services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Scope of this Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all apps and games developed and published by Emanvidmaker. It governs how we handle your data when you download, install, or use our Services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Personal Information

We may collect information that identifies you directly, such as:

  • Name and email address (e.g., for account creation, customer support, or newsletters).
  • Payment information for in-app purchases (processed securely by third-party payment processors).

Non-Personal Information

We may collect data automatically to improve our Services, including:

  • Device information (e.g., model, OS, unique identifiers, IP address).
  • Gameplay data (levels completed, in-game achievements)
  • Analytics data to improve user experience
  • Usage data (e.g., gameplay activity, session duration, crash reports).
  • Advertising identifiers (e.g., Google Ad ID).

Data from Third-Party Platforms

If you link third-party accounts (e.g., Google Play, Facebook), we may collect data such as your profile name and avatar, subject to their privacy policies.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Game functionality (e.g., saving progress, leaderboards)
  • Improving the Game through analytics and bug fixes
  • To operate and improve our apps and games.
  • To process in-app purchases securely.
  • To show contextual or personalized ads based on your preferences.
  • To analyze user behavior and enhance gameplay experiences.
  • To provide customer support and respond to inquiries.
  • Showing contextual and personalized ads

4. Third-Party Services

We may share data with trusted third-party providers for:

  • Analytics: To monitor app performance and improve features.

    • Steam (Privacy Policy)

  • Payment Processing: To handle secure in-app purchases.

    • Google Play (Privacy Policy)
    • Steam (Privacy Policy)

These partners process data under their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

5. Data Retention

We retain collected information only as long as necessary to provide the Game’s features and comply with legal obligations.

6. Your Rights

  • You can request access to, correction, or deletion of your data by contacting us at emanvidmaker@gmail.com.
  • Opt-out options: Adjust your device settings to disable certain data collection, such as ad personalization.

7. Legal Basis for Data Processing (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union, we process your data based on:

  • Consent: For personalized ads or optional features.
  • Contract: To provide you with requested Services.
  • Legitimate Interests: For app improvements and fraud prevention.

8. Your Privacy Rights

Under GDPR (EU Residents):

  • Access and Correction: Request access to your data or correct inaccuracies.
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("Right to be Forgotten").
  • Object to Processing: Opt out of data processing, including for marketing purposes.
  • Portability: Receive a copy of your data in a portable format.

Under CCPA (California Residents):

  • Know: Request details about the categories and specific data collected.
  • Delete: Ask us to delete your data, subject to legal exceptions.
  • Opt Out: Prevent the sale of your personal information (we do not sell data).

To exercise your rights, contact us at emanvidmaker@gmail.com.

9. Opt-Out Options

  • Personalized Ads: Disable ad personalization via your device settings:
    • On Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads → “Opt-out of Ads Personalization.”
  • Tracking and Analytics: Use the app’s settings (if available) to disable optional data collection.

10. Children’s Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 in some jurisdictions). If we learn that we have collected data from a child without proper consent, we will delete it promptly.

11. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or as required by law.

12. Data Transfers

If you access our Services from outside your home country your data may be transferred and processed in a country with different data protection laws. We ensure such transfers comply with applicable legal requirements.

13. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, no system is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee complete security.

14. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted with a new effective date, and significant changes will be communicated through the app.
